Secure Connection and Privacy Assurance

Techducat Limited is committed to safeguarding your privacy on the "6°" mobile application (the "App"). We want to ensure that you have all the necessary information about how we protect your privacy.

1. Secure Connection: When you use the App, your connection to our relay server at https://8.222.202.85:3001 is secured through HTTPS. This ensures that your data is transmitted securely over the network, enhancing your privacy and data protection.

2. Unique Identifier: We assign you a unique identifier on the peer-to-peer network, created using SHA-256 encryption, which generates a 64-character string that cannot be reversed (decrypted). This identifier serves as your identity on the network and is kept secure.

3. Contact List: We encrypt your contact list using SHA-256 encryption, which generates a list of 64-character strings that cannot be reversed (decrypted) just like your own identifier. These identifiers serve as the identities of your peers on the network and are shared with other peers by sending to the relay server at https://8.222.202.85:3001 through an HTTP connection.

4. Contact List Usage:The encrypted identifiers derived from the Contact List serve as the identities of your peers on the network and are shared with other peers by sending them to the relay server at https://8.222.202.85:3001 through an HTTP connection.

5. Privacy Assurance: We take your privacy seriously. We do not disclose your unique identifier (SHA-256 string) to any third parties, as it is designed to be unrecoverable. Your identity remains secure and anonymous on our network.

6. Minimal Information: The only required information for using the App is your cellphone number, which is solely used for creating your unique identifier. We collect minimal personal information, and we do not intentionally collect any additional personal information from children under 13. The App is available for users of all ages.

7. Security Measures: We have implemented robust security measures to protect your information, including your unique identifier. The use of HTTPS ensures that your data is encrypted during transmission, maintaining the security and anonymity of your identity on our network.

8. Changes to Our Policies: We may update our policies to reflect changes in our practices or for other reasons. You will be notified of any material changes through the App.
